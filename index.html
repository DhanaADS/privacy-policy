Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2025

At DhanaADS, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines what information we collect and how we use it when you use our Gmail integration dashboard.

What we collect:

  • Authenticated Gmail account email address
  • Read-only metadata of your recent emails (subject, from, snippet)

How we use it:

  • To show email previews on your private CRM dashboard
  • No data is shared, sold, or stored externally

Permissions:

We only request https://www.googleapis.com/auth/gmail.readonly scope to display unread email previews within your dashboard. We do NOT store or send email data outside your browser.

Data Security:

All access tokens are stored securely via Supabase authentication and expire automatically.
